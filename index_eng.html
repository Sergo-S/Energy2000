About company

Since 1999, the Research and Production Enterprise "Energy 2000" has been working in the field of development and production of military simulators. The company specializes in the development and manufacture of simulators for mechanized (tank) units, air defense and artillery units, and flight simulators.

Among the employees of the enterprise there are six candidates of technical and military sciences. Employees regularly publish the results of scientific research in scientific-theoretical and scientific-practical journals published in Ukraine on the development and use of training systems in combat training of the Ground Forces.

Simulators developed by the enterprise:

  • driving simulators and complex simulators for tanks, combat vehicles;
  • infantry, armored personnel carriers;
  • simulators for motorized rifle and tank platoons and companies;
  • simulators for units of military and object air defense;
  • simulators for operators of anti-tank systems;
  • fire control simulators for artillery units;
  • simulators for helicopter and aircraft crews;
  • combined arms training center.
